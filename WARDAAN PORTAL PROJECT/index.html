Welcome to our WARDAAN Portal

WARDAAN is an abbreviation for Without Any Rush Doctors Are Available Next. The Basic aim of our platform is Automation of appointments in medical sector. This platform will reduce the gap between patients and hospitals providing each of them with the facility to update appointments and check the details on the go with the comfort of home

Our Functionalities

WARDAAN Site Provides:

Booking Appointments for any and all Hospitals

Here, You can directly book any hospital or clinic 24*7 without visiting particular hospital.

Thanks to Princy and Tommy(Shayar) to make it possible

Adjusting, Canceling, Postponing Appointments

You can directly postpone or adjust your appointment without visiting to hospital or clinic.

Again,Thanks to Princy and Tommy(Shayar) to make it possible

Having a Place To store the Documents

After generating UPID Number (Unique Patient ID Number), you can store your clinical reports and documents for future purpose

Again,Thanks to Princy and Tommy(Shayar) to make it possible

Comparing the pricing of different Hospitals at a single place

You can compare the price of different hospitals and clinic for the same services so that you can choose the hospital wisely.

Again,Thanks to Princy and Tommy(Shayar) to make it possible
